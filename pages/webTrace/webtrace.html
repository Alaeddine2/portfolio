App Moduls

WebTrace contains multiple moduls:

The Parc

A small ERP that allows users to manage there drivers, cars, stocks, bulls, translactions and more.

Real Time Tracer

Users can track mobile objects (like cars,...) in real time, or they can review its history of activities and path.

Account manager

It's a configuration part that allows users or admins to manage their devices, accounts and sub-accounts.

Tank tracer

A dahsbord that allows users to monitor their tanks in real time its temperator, values, pression and more.. also he can control the tanks via our IOT equipements, so he can stop the consumption, fix the internal temperator, resist the system...

Webtrace dahboard

it's a collection of data taken from the other parts of the project.

WebTrace Reporting

A section for Exporting reports about every everyThing.

Application Concept

WebTrace is a package of modules that offers a multiple of services using a collection of GPS/IOT systems and cartographic data.

Based on plurality of our IOT solution now our webTrace can manage and control cars, tanks, people, pets and more... our solution offers pricision and effecacity.

Application sub-features per module

Features of Parc module:

  • Driver managment.
  • Car management.
  • File management.
  • Stock management...

Features of Suivi module (Real Time Tracer):

  • Real time status monotoring.
  • Real time tracking.
  • ReTrack the object activities.
  • Configuration of a interval of the object cerculation...

Features of Account manager:

  • Account management.
  • Departement management.
  • Alert configuration.
  • Configuration of SMS/EMAIL sender...

Features of Tank tracer:

  • Status of using data.
  • Status of energie consomation.
  • Status of object using.

Features of Report:

  • Exporting reports of all data.
  • Exportion dynamic reports.
  • Exporting statistic reports.
  • Exporting mission reports...
